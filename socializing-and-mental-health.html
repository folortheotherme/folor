Socializing and Mental Health

  • 13 May 2018
  • by Vishakha Damania
When you take a good look at the entire universe, you will tend to notice that everything seems to work in tandem with one another.

Ever watched a herd of cows going around? You will notice that they are always closely followed by egrets and their relationship is one that is mutually beneficial to each other. In this article, you will get to see the reasons why women should always esteem the interpersonal relationship that they can build online. You will get to see the positive impact which such relationships bring in the long run and hopefully you will get to understand the need to be drastic in getting a few friends if you have been content with being alone. Let's examine some of the reasons that necessitate interpersonal relationships.

It helps to aid your career

It is no longer news that women have always been marginalized in the workplace and restricted from holding key positions in society. In order to put an end to this trend, did you know what women did? They built strong interpersonal relationships and bonded. You can read up many victories achieved by the feminist movement at the source given at the end of this article. But the key thing is that women could only achieve these successes because they bonded. There is immense strength in numbers at every time, and when you begin to build close interpersonal relationships online, you are automatically given a boost in your career pursuits.

It aids healthy living

Scientific research that was once carried out, showed that many elderly citizens were usually victims of health challenges like Alzheimer’s, depression, and others due to the intense loneliness they suffer in their old age. On the other hand, Elderly people who had loved ones around them to care for them and also speak to them often lived longer and enjoyed a cleaner bill of health. I give this example to show that even for women below 40, building online interpersonal relationships is vital. There is a whole lot of good that can come from building relationships with other people you meet on websites like Facebook, Instagram, and other websites. If you are a member of a church that has an online presence for example and built a network of friends there, it would be easier for them to spot out changes in the frequency of your online activity. When these changes are spotted out, they can become a strong support group that will help you to regain your health fully by always offering helpful comments, posts and maybe calling you via Skype. In most occasions, the online support group may raise funds for you on GoFundMe.com out of the depths of their love for you even if you did not request for help.

It helps to boost your confidence

Every woman below 40 at some point in her life will suffer a huge episode of low self-confidence. Some suffer this after a terrible ordeal such as a breakup, divorce, rape etc. Irrespective of the situation in which you suffer self-confidence, you will need an online support system to help you get back on your feet. If you are in college, you would need to build strong sister bonds with other females so that you can be able to walk as one and fight as one against every form of danger that may lurk. For women who are finding it difficult as starting out single mothers, it is vital to have a support system in form of interpersonal relationship with other moms online so as to be strengthened. This brings to mind Bad Moms, a 2017 film which chronicled the antics of three mothers who were trying to be the best mothers for their kids. In the film, it was the interpersonal relationship they built with each other that ensured that they did not break down from the pressure of parenting. There are many online groups where you can meet other young moms and get the necessary tips for your child.

Having examined some of the benefits of building good interpersonal relationships, it is vital for you to know some tips that will aid you in identifying the right support group for you.

Tip 1: Build with someone that shares your values

Trust me; there is nothing more terrible than building an interpersonal relationship with someone that does not share the same values with you even online. Always be quick to taking note of salient points about every individual that you meet online, and never judge the character based on what they always post. If you notice that they are quick to speak so ill about their friends or family online and in personal comments, they will also be quick to post negative stuff about you in the long run.

Tip 2: Build with someone who combines passion with empathy.

Most women are at times fascinated about other women who seem to be passionate about their careers. However, you should not be like the rest. Rather, identify someone who has empathy because she would be able to forgo something in her life in order to help you out when you reach out for help online. Remember that it is not necessarily about the net worth of their fortunes that you should be attracted to. Rather, the net worth of their ability to care. If you notice that she is always online to provide help for you, that is a great friend.

Tip 3: Do not trust too quickly

Oprah Winfrey is one of the most influential public speakers of all times and one priceless advice which she gifts to women is that they should not trust so easily. At some occasions, you may meet this wonderful person online and wonder, “Where have you been all my life?” This thought may be inspired by the fact that you both seem to complete each other’s posts so easily and find out that you share the same Pinterest likes and Instagram preferences. It is vital that you do not be fooled by this. Don’t go chatting online with this person and telling everything about you and your family because she may turn out to use the information against you in a future post or through a scam. Understand that there are many scam activities going online at the moment and this means you should not be easy with any of your financial details.

There are more tips that can also help you in building great interpersonal relationships but the most important thing to note is that you should learn to respect the sanctity of the person online. Respect the person's values and also endeavor to give more in the relationship, whether online or offline. When you engage in this, you will see that it becomes easy for you to overcome challenging situations as well as living a great life.
